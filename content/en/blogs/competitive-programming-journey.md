Competitive programming has been one of the most defining experiences of my university years. It all started in my first year at Zagazig University when a senior student introduced me to the ICPC community.

## The Beginning

I remember the first time I tried solving a problem on Codeforces. It was a simple "A" problem, and it took me almost an hour to solve. But the feeling of seeing "Accepted" for the first time was incredible. That moment hooked me into the world of competitive programming.

## Growing Through Challenges

Over the next few years, I dedicated countless hours to practicing algorithms and data structures. From basic sorting and searching to advanced topics like segment trees, dynamic programming, and graph algorithms — each topic opened a new world of problem-solving possibilities.

### Key Milestones

1. **First University Contest Win:** In my second year, our team won the university-level ICPC contest for the first time. This was a huge confidence boost.

2. **ACPC Qualification:** We qualified for the African and Arab Collegiate Programming Championship in 2022, and then again in 2023 and 2024. Each year we improved our standing.

3. **Teaching and Mentoring:** I started mentoring younger students, which surprisingly improved my own understanding of algorithms. Teaching greedy algorithms and C++ STL became my regular sessions.

## Lessons Learned

- **Consistency beats intensity:** Solving 2-3 problems daily is better than 20 problems in one weekend
- **Think before you code:** Spending time on paper before writing code saves hours of debugging
- **Collaborate and learn:** Team practice exposes you to different thinking patterns
- **Read editorial solutions:** Even for problems you've solved — there's always a more elegant approach

## What's Next

I plan to continue participating in competitive programming contests and to expand the ICPC community at Zagazig University. The problem-solving skills I've gained have been invaluable in my software engineering career, and I want to help more students discover this path.
